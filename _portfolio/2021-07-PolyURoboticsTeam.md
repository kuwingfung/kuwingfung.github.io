I joined PolyU Robotics Team in my first year and retired after the third year playing in Robocon. Each year we were given 500k HKD funding to build 2 robots to play in ABU Robocon HK (Biggest inter-university robotics event in Asia & HK). I contributed in 4 robot developments over the past 3 years. Playing in Robotics Team has gained me 1) experience participating in large practical robot development projects 2) insights in state of the art technologies 3) resilience when working under a tight deadline 4) ability in problem solving without any help. In addition, I also learnt to pick up any skills in a short period.
