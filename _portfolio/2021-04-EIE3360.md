In this work, we developed a 3D first person bomberman game in Unity. The game has a similar mechanism as the original bomberman game. We extended our work to a multiplayer mode that support both computer and mobile platforms. This work was further integrated into virtual reality platform and submitted to i-Space Virtual Reality Contest.
