description: "Project for the course Machine Learning in Cyber-Security at PolyU. In this work, we conduct a performance analysis on Spam Email Detection with machine learning methods from research papers. We compare the methodology and results between XGBoost, LSTMs, and GRUs by reimplementing the research papers' methods. Moreover, we suggested a novel approach to improve the existing methods by reducing training parameters and thus increasing the training efficiency."
