My research interests are Computer Vision and Deep Image Synthesis, and I enjoy exploring SOTA technologies in 2D image and 3D vision areas. My goal is to create tools to free human's creativity and visualize their mind, produce content with ease.

Previously, I have received Bachelor of Science (Hons) Degree in Internet and Multimedia Technologies at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) with first class honor in 2022. I am glad that I was involved in RoboCon and VR contests during my undergraduate study, in which I acquired technical skills and developed the mindset of a professional engineer. The academic environment also raised my interest in research. I was lucky to work with [Prof. Bonnie Law](http://www.eie.polyu.edu.hk/~nflaw/) in the Undergraduate Research and Innovation Scheme (URIS) programme to learn about the creation of knowledge. In addition, I also had an opportunity to contribute in [Prof. Wan-Chi Siu](http://www.eie.polyu.edu.hk/~wcsiu/)'s research group and produced an academic paper.
